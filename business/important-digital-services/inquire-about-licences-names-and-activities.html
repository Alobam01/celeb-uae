    Verify business licences

    Several channels and platforms can be used to get information about a business licence. These include websites of DEDs in each emirate and National Economic Register.
    U.Ask

    Verify business licences

    The following links will help you check business activities registered and licences issued in the UAE:

    • Inquiry by business name or business activity - Unified Commercial Registration System - Ministry of Economy
    • Inquire about commercial companies licences - Ministry of Economy
    • Inquire about economic licences - Abu Dhabi
    • Search trade name - Dubai Economy and Tourism
    • Search for a company licence - Dubai Economy and Tourism
    • Trade name search - Economic Development Department – Sharjah
    • Trade name inquiry - Department of Economic Development - Ajman
    • Search tade licence - Umm Al Quwain
    • Rukhsati app – Fujairah Municipality on iTunes and Google Play.
